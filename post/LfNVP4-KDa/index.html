Principles of Counting

加法原理和乘法原理

Addition Principle

If AAA and BBB are finite sets and A∩B=∅A \cap B =\emptysetA∩B=∅, that is AAA and BBB are disjoint, then ∣A∪B∣=∣A∣+∣B∣|A \cup B| =|A|+|B|∣A∪B∣=∣A∣+∣B∣.

Multiplication Principle

If AAA and BBB are finite sets, then ∣A×B∣=∣A∣∣B∣|A \times B| =|A||B|∣A×B∣=∣A∣∣B∣.
Recall that A×B={(a,b):a∈A,b∈B}A \times B =\{(a,b):a\in A, b\in B \}A×B={(a,b):a∈A,b∈B}.
